Enable Notifications from Oracle Support

Oracle Support might want to chat with you about your open service requests. While you are signed into My Oracle Support, we can pop-up a message to let you know your engineer wants to chat. To allow these pop-up notifications, do two things:

  1. Click Enable Notifications below
  2. When the browser offers a second prompt for you to allow/show notifications, click that button too.
Once this is done, you will see a notification whenever Oracle Support attempts to contact you by chat while you are logged into My Oracle Support.
